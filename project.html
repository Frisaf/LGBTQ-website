LGBTQ+

Your digital encyclopedia for everything LGBTQ+ related!

The letters in the acronym "LGBTQ" stands for "Lesbian Gay Trans Queer". Another shorter version of LGBTQ is LGBT, which leaves out queer. You may also come across other versions such as "LGBTQIA+" and "2SLGBTQIA+" where "I" stands for "Intersex", "A" for "Asexual" and "2S" for "Two Spirit". The plus may be inluded in all acronyms and indicates that there are more than just those letters.

Most common sexual orientations

"Sexual orientation - a person's identity in relation to the gender or genders to which they are sexually attracted; the fact of being heterosexual, homosexual, etc." (Google)

Not to be confused with romantic attraction. The difference between sexual and romantic attraction is that sexual attraction is who you'd want to have the dirty with while romantic attraction is attraction that makes people desire romantic contact or interaction with someone else.

A list of the most common sexual orientations

  • Heterosexuality - Sexual attraction to people of a different gender than their own.
  • Homosexual - Sexual attraction to people of the same gender as their own. This may refer to both gay and lesbian people.
