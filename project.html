The letters in the acronym "LGBTQ" stands for "Lesbian Gay Trans Queer". Another shorter version of LGBTQ is LGBT, which leaves out queer. You may also come across other versions such as "LGBTQIA+" and "2SLGBTQIA+" where "I" stands for "Intersex", "A" for "Asexual" and "2S" for "Two Spirit". The plus may be inluded in all acronyms and indicates that there are more than just those letters.

Hello! My name is Ash, a trans masculine pansexual person from Sweden and I'm the creator behind this project.
